You can make a difference by joining us as a volunteer or becoming a sponsor. Explore the opportunities below and complete the form to get started.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a direct impact:

  • Tutor children and youth - Help with homework and subject tutoring
  • Digital literacy instruction - Teach basic computer skills
  • Event coordination - Assist in organizing educational events
  • Mentorship programs - Guide students in career planning
  • Fundraising activities - Support our financial sustainability
  • Administrative support - Help with office tasks and coordination

Sponsor Opportunities

Support our mission through corporate or individual sponsorship:

  • Educational scholarships - Fund school fees for deserving students
  • Technology infrastructure - Support digital literacy programs
  • Learning materials - Provide books and educational resources
  • Program sponsorship - Adopt a specific program or school
  • B-BBEE partnerships - Align with corporate social responsibility goals
