Who We Are

The Hope for Tomorrow Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to creating lasting change in underprivileged communities. We believe that every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and thrive in a supportive environment. Through our initiatives in education, health, and empowerment, we bring hope to tomorrow's generation.

Organisation Overview

Established in 2018, the Hope for Tomorrow Foundation is a registered Section 18A non-profit organization that supports underprivileged children's education in the townships and rural areas of Gauteng. Founded by former teachers Nomsa Mthembu and Pieter van der Merwe, the organization was created to address post-apartheid educational inequality.

Our Work Focuses on Three Main Initiatives:

Digital Literacy Workshops

Equipping learners with essential technology skills for the modern world.

Scholarships for Grades 10-12

Enabling learners to access further education opportunities.

After-School Tutoring

Offering extra support in Afrikaans, English, and mathematics.

Our Mission

To provide underprivileged youth with resources, education, and mentorship that end cycles of poverty and pave the way for postsecondary education and employment.

Our Vision

A South Africa where all children have equal access to education, irrespective of their background.
