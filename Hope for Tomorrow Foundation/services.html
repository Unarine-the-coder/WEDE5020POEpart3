Our Services

At the Hope for Tomorrow Foundation, our work is centered on breaking cycles of poverty and creating opportunities for underprivileged children in Gauteng's townships and rural areas. Since our establishment in 2018, we have designed programs that not only address immediate educational needs but also build long-term pathways for success.

Digital Literacy Workshops

We run digital literacy workshops to equip learners with essential computer and technology skills. In a world driven by technology, access to digital knowledge helps children prepare for the modern workplace and gives them the tools to succeed in school and beyond.

  • Basic computer skills training
  • Internet safety education
  • Digital creativity programs
  • Technology access provision

High School Scholarships (Grades 10-12)

Through our scholarship program, we provide financial support for learners in Grades 10-12. These scholarships give deserving students the chance to complete their high school education and pursue postsecondary studies or career opportunities.

  • Tuition fee coverage
  • School supplies provision
  • University application support
  • Career guidance counseling

After-School Tutoring

Our after-school tutoring program provides additional support in core subjects, ensuring that every child has the academic support they need to thrive.

Subjects Offered:

  • Afrikaans
  • English
  • Mathematics

These sessions are led by volunteer tutors ranging from college students to retired teachers.
